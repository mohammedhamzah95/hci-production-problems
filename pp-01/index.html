Cinzel Medium & Fauna One

Cinzel is a typeface that is inspired by the Latin alphabet and similar to the Trajan font discussed in class. Cinzel is best used as headlines and has an ancient look but with a contemporary feel.

Fauna One is a contemporary font that was designed by Eduardo Tunni. It can be used in headlines as well as paragraphs. It is composed of "low contrast strokes" that are similar to traditional serifs.
